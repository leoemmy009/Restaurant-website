Our Story

Tradition & Passion Since 1985

Bella Cucina was founded in 1985 by Giovanni and Maria Rossi, bringing the authentic flavors of their hometown in Naples to the heart of the city. What started as a small family trattoria has grown into a beloved culinary destination, known for its warm atmosphere and traditional Italian cuisine.

Today, their son Marco Chealini continues the family tradition, using the same recipes passed down through generations while adding his own creative touch. We take pride in sourcing the finest ingredients, with many imported directly from Italy.

Our chefs are trained in the art of Italian cooking, ensuring every dish that leaves our kitchen meets the highest standards of authenticity and quality.
